About Us


AndyLux Hotel & Suites is a premier hospitality establishment located in the vibrant city of Lagos, Nigeria. Renowned for its opulent accommodations, impeccable service, and strategic location, the hotel has become a symbol of luxury and comfort in the region. With a commitment to excellence, AndyLux Hotel & Suites offers a world-class experience to both leisure and business travelers.

AndyLux Hotel & Suites boasts a range of elegantly appointed rooms and suites, each designed to provide the utmost comfort and relaxation. Guests can choose from a variety of room types, all of which exude opulence and style.

The hotel offers a selection of gourmet dining options, serving both local and international cuisines. With expert chefs and a focus on culinary excellence, guests can indulge in an exquisite dining experience. AndyLux Hotel & Suites offers an array of modern facilities designed to cater to both leisure and business travelers.
