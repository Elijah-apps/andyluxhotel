Our Rooms

No matter which room you choose, our luxury hotel promises a world of comfort, elegance, and impeccable service for an unforgettable stay.

Presidential Suite

Experience the pinnacle of luxury in our Presidential Suite. This expansive, opulent space offers breathtaking panoramic views, a private butler, a spacious living area, and a lavish bedroom with a king-size bed. Pamper yourself in the marble-clad bathroom with a Jacuzzi and a rainforest shower.

Grand Deluxe

Wake up to the soothing sound of waves in our Grand Deluxe Room. Enjoy a spacious balcony overlooking the beautiful city, a plush king-size bed, and a beautifully appointed bathroom with a deep soaking tub and premium amenities.

Royal Family Suite

Designed for families seeking both space and style, our Royal Family Suite boasts two separate bedrooms, a cozy living area, and a private terrace. Kids will adore the themed décor, and parents will appreciate the in-suite dining options.

Executive Club Room

Elevate your stay with exclusive access to our Executive Club Lounge. These rooms feature modern design, a comfortable work area, and access to complimentary breakfast, afternoon tea, and evening cocktails in the Club Lounge.

Junior Suite

Our Junior Suites are perfect for business travelers or couples seeking a bit more space. Enjoy a separate living area, a comfortable king-size bed, and a marble-clad bathroom with a deep bathtub.

Honeymoon Suite

Celebrate your love in our Honeymoon Suite. Enjoy romantic touches like a four-poster bed, a private terrace, and a heart-shaped Jacuzzi in the beautifully designed suite.
